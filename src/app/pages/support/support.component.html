Your Support Matters

Hello fellow Naruto enthusiasts and developers! The Dattebayo API is a labor of love, crafted to bring the magic of the Naruto universe to your fingertips. It's currently deployed on a free plan on Render due to financial constraints, but with your support, we can take it to new heights!

Ways to Support

1. Share Your Ideas

We're always eager to hear your thoughts and ideas for making Dattebayo API even better. Whether it's a new feature, a UI enhancement, or a fresh collection to add, your ideas can shape the future of this API. Share your suggestions on our GitHub Front/ API or connect with us on Discord to discuss your ideas.

2. Contribute with Pull Requests

If you're a developer, you can directly contribute to the growth of Dattebayo API by submitting pull requests on our GitHub Front/ API . Whether it's fixing a bug, improving documentation, or adding new features, your expertise can make a significant impact on the community's experience.

3. Financial Support

While the API is currently on a free plan due to financial reasons, your financial support can help us expand its capabilities and ensure its availability for the long term. Even a small contribution can go a long way in covering operational costs and future enhancements.
